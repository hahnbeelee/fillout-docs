## How to edit responses

Go to your form's `Results` tab. Then, **right-click** on the input you need to `Edit`.

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2026-01-07at6.46.28PM.png"
  alt="Screenshot 2026 01 07 At 6 46 28 PM"
  title="Screenshot 2026 01 07 At 6 46 28 PM"
  style={{ width:"99%" }}
/>

You’ll be redirected to the form pre-filled with the respondent’s answers. Edit any input there and click `Submit`.

![PDF Viewer](/images/guides/image-7.png)

You can also send the **link** of this pre-filled form to the respondent in case they want to edit their answers. Simply **right-click** on a cell and choose `Copy link`.

<Check>
  Editing form submissions automatically updates data synced through [**integrations**](/integrations). This will update existing records in Airtable or Notion, send new alerts on Slack, update Dropbox folders, and etc.
</Check>
