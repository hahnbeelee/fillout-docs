## How to edit responses

Go to your form's `Results` tab. Then, right-click on the input you need to `Edit` it.

![Screenshot 2025-10-16 at 3.13.06 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-16at3.13.06PM.png)

You’ll be redirected to the form pre-filled with the respondent’s answers. Edit any input there and click `Submit`.

![PDF Viewer](/images/guides/image-7.png)

You can also send the **link** of this pre-filled form to the respondent in case they want to edit their answers.

<Check>
  Editing form submissions automatically updates data synced through [**integrations**](/integrations). This will update existing records in Airtable or Notion, send new alerts on Slack, update Dropbox folders, and etc.
</Check>
