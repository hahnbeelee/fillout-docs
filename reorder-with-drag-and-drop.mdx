## How to reorder pages

Simply drag pages in the bottom toolbar and position them in the order you would like the form pages to be shown.

<Frame>
  ![Reorder pages using drag and drop](/images/guides/image-92.gif)
</Frame>

<Note>
  **Note:** You cannot put any page before a **Cover** page and after an **Ending** page. Reordering is also disabled if you have added [branching](/page-logic) logic to your form. To reorder forms with branching logic, see [page logic](/page-logic).
</Note>

## How to reorder questions or field options

This can be applied to **Multiple choice**, **Dropdown**, **Checkboxes**, and **Picture choice** field types.

**Multiple choice**

Drag the options in the center of the screen to reorder choices.

<Frame>
  ![uides](/images/guides/image-93.gif)
</Frame>

**Dropdown and multi-select**

Click the `settings icon` to open the right-hand panel. Drag options there to reorder them.

<Frame>
  ![Reorder dropdown options](/images/guides/image-94.gif)
</Frame>

**Checkboxes**

Hover towards the right side of the checkbox option you want to reorder then drag the 6 dots to where you want the option to be placed.

<Frame>
  ![Reorder checkboxes](/images/guides/image-95.gif)
</Frame>

**Picture choices**

Simply drag the picture of choice you want to move and drop where you want it to be placed.

<Frame>
  ![Reorder picture choices](/images/guides/image-96.gif)
</Frame>
