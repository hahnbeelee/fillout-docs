## How to add a cover page

Click `+ Add page` followed by `Cover`. Then, enter the text.

![Screenshot 2025-10-01 at 4.50.00â€¯PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at4.50.00PM.png)

When the form is opened, the text will **animate smoothly**. Through the left-hand panel, you can customize your cover page by:

- Editing the button text
- Adding an image above the title
- Changing the custom cover page [layout](https://www.fillout.com/help/layout)

To customize the **design** of your page and add a [logo](/logo), click `Theme` in the upper left are. Learn more [here](/themes).
