## Set up 2FA

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open settings">
    On the upper left corner of your Fillout dashboard, click your `Account name` followed by `Settings`.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-09-23at2.36.57PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-09-23 at 2.36.57 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-09-23 at 2.36.57 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"35%" }}
    />

    <Check>
      This feature is available on all plans including the Free tier.
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to account settings">
    In the **My account page**, `Enable 2FA`.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-09-23at2.26.48PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-09-23 at 2.26.48 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-09-23 at 2.26.48 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"82%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify with an authenticator app">
    Scan the QR code in the popup, and input the **6-digit code** generated by your authentication app or password manager. Then, click `Enable`.

    <img
      src="/images/image.png"
      alt="image.png"
      title="image.png"
      style={{ width:"47%" }}
    />

    That's it! 2FA is now enabled. You'll be asked for a 6-digit code from your authentication app or password manager when logging in on a new device or browser.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Require 2FA for all org members

In **General** settings, toggle the `Enforce two-factor authentication in this organization` switch.

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2025-09-23at2.37.43PM.png"
  alt="Screenshot 2025-09-23 at 2.37.43 PM.png"
  title="Screenshot 2025-09-23 at 2.37.43 PM.png"
  style={{ width:"79%" }}
/>

<Note>
  This feature is available on the **Enterprise Tier 1** plan. Learn more [**here**](https://www.fillout.com/enterprise).
</Note>
