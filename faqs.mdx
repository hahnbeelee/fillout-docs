## <Icon icon="lightbulb" size={24} /> Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What kinds of forms can I build with Fillout?">
    You can make surveys, quizzes, lead generation or intake forms, contact forms, scheduling or booking pages, registration forms, and many more. Start from a blank canvas or use a template.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use AI to build my forms?">
    Yes, Fillout has an [AI Form Builder](https://www.fillout.com/ai-form-builder), [AI Survey Maker](https://www.fillout.com/ai-survey-maker), and [AI Quiz Builder](https://www.fillout.com/ai-quiz-maker) that generates forms based on a prompt or by importing files including existing forms.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I copy questions and other elements in bulk?" description="Yes, you can press `Shift` on your keyboard and select multiple questions and elements and copy them.">
    
  </Accordion>
