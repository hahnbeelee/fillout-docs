## What is user login?

User login lets you control who can access your app. With Zite, you can set up a secure login page, manage users, and control access without writing any code.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add User Login">
    Type <b>Add user login</b> in your Zite chat. Zite will automatically create a complete user login backend and integrate it into your app.
    <Frame>
      ![Zite chat with 'Add user login' prompt](/images/auth-step-1.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Your New Login Page">
    Zite instantly generates a login screen for your appâ€”no coding required.
    <Frame>
      ![Zite login page preview](/images/auth-step-9.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Your Login Page">
    Click <b>Edit</b> to personalize your login page. Modify the logo, background, and text, or use AI for edits. Click **Save** and **Sign In** to your app.
    <Frame>
      ![Zite login page customization](/images/auth-step-10.png)
    </Frame>
    <Tip>
      Incorporate your brand colors and logo for a professional appearance.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Users">
    Access the <b>Users</b> tab to view, edit, or delete users. Manage access effortlessly.
    <Frame>
      ![Zite user management tab](/images/auth-step-4.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Control Access and Login Methods">
    Select login methods: Magic link or Google login.  
    Then, define access levels:
    1. Invite-only
    2. Open to anyone
    3. Company-specific
    <Frame>
      ![Zite access control settings](/images/auth-step-5.png)
    </Frame>
    <Note>
      For company-specific access, input your domain and save the settings.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
Setup complete. You can now invite users to your app.
</Check>
