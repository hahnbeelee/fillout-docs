**Elements** refer to all text and images generated on your app, which includes headers, descriptions, buttons, banners, section backgrounds, dividers, hyperlinks, icons, and input fields.

## Style elements in your app

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select an element">
    Click `Edit` in the upper right corner followed by the element you'd like to customize.

    ![Screenshot 2025-08-20 at 2.51.53 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-20at2.51.53PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type a prompt">
    Enter the change you'd like to make to the element in the chat box. In our example, we requested one of the trending articles featured to be replaced.

    ![Screenshot 2025-08-20 at 2.53.15 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-20at2.53.15PM.png)

    **or Use the manual editor**

    Here, you can adjust font size, font weight, text color, background color, spacing, corner rounding, and shadows.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-08-20at3.21.53PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-08-20 at 3.21.53 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-08-20 at 3.21.53 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"75%" }}
    />

    You can also delete elements directly from this panel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Undo changes

Simply click `Undo` on the change you'd like to revert. Once undone, this action cannot be reversed.
