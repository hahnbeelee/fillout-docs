## How to sync data to Airtable

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to Airtable">
    Enter a prompt in the chat box or choose `Airtable` from the Integrations popup in the chat box.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-24at2.11.48PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 2.11.48 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 2.11.48 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"42%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a base">
    You can use an existing base or generate a new one with the help of AI.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-24at1.58.20PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 1.58.20 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 1.58.20 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"68%" }}
    />

    Tell Zite how you want to use Airtable in your app.

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 1.59.07 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-24at1.59.07PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test your integration">
    Use the chat box or the respective buttons in your app to ensure everything's working properly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize the design">
    [Modify the theme](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/design) or [style specific elements](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/selective-edits) your app to personalize it or match it with your brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy and share">
    Click `Publish` in the upper right corner to [**share**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/sharing) or [**embed**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/embedding) your app.
  </Step>
</Steps>
