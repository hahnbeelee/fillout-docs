## Common use cases

By connecting Airtable, you can easily build apps for things like:

- **Inventory Management:** Monitor stock levels and new items
- **Directory Listings:** Store and manage information
- **Project Planning:** Manage existing and new projects
- **Content Scheduling:** Track statuses and updates as a team
- **Event Management:** Monitor guest RSVPs and supplier statuses
- **CRM Tracking:** Manage leads, clients, or deals
