## How to automate notifications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to Slack">
    In the chat box, request your app to be integrated with Slack or click <Icon icon="slack" iconType="solid" />.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-12-17at2.33.11PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025 12 17 At 2 33 11 PM"
      title="Screenshot 2025 12 17 At 2 33 11 PM"
      style={{ width:"44%" }}
    />

    Then, sign in to your Slack account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a channel">
    Choose a public channel from the dropdown. You can easily change the channel later on through here.

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 4.06.21 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-24at4.06.21PM.png)

    For private channels, go to the specific channel on Slack and simply type `@Fillout` to enable notifications there. Then, choose this channel in the dropdown.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-24at4.27.30PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 4.27.30 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 4.27.30 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"47%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your use case">
    Tell Zite how you want to use Slack in your app. In our example, we'll send a notification to the development team every time a new bug is reported.

    ![Screenshot 2025 12 17 At 2 19 23 PM](/images/Screenshot2025-12-17at2.19.23PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy and share">
    Use the chat box or the respective buttons in your app to ensure everything's working properly.

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 4.23.32 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-24at4.23.32PM.png)

    Click `Publish` in the upper right corner to [**share**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/sharing) or [**embed**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/embedding) your app.

    [Modify the theme](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/design) or [style specific elements](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/selective-edits) your app to personalize it or match it with your brand.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common use cases

By connecting Slack, you can easily build apps for things like:

- **Support Tickets:** Notify your team everytime a new ticket comes in
- **Order Confirmation:** Notify your fulfillment team of new orders
- **Meeting Bookings:** Receive alerts in real-time upon new bookings
- **Bug Reporting:** Get alerts everytime there is a new bug or technical issue
