## How to integrate OpenAI

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to OpenAI">
    In the chat box, request your app to be integrated with OpenAI or click the **apps**.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-12-17at2.33.11PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025 12 17 At 2 33 11 PM"
      title="Screenshot 2025 12 17 At 2 33 11 PM"
      style={{ width:"44%" }}
    />

    Then, sign in to your OpenAI or ChatGPT account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a version">
    Choose your preferred version in the dropdown.

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 6.13.03 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-24at6.13.03PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter use case">
    Tell Zite how you want to use OpenAI in your app. In our example, we'll publish a post to the community.

    ![Screenshot 2025 12 17 At 2 19 30 PM](/images/Screenshot2025-12-17at2.19.30PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy and share">
    Use the chat box or the respective buttons in your app to ensure everything's working properly.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-24at6.20.23PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 6.20.23 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 6.20.23 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"57%" }}
    />

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 6.21.22 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-24at6.21.22PM.png)

    Click `Publish` in the upper right corner to [**share**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/sharing) or [**embed**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/embedding) your app.

    [Modify the theme](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/design) or [style specific elements](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/selective-edits) your app to personalize it or match it with your brand.
  </Step>
</Steps>
