This guide shows you how to create a CRM dashboard connected to Google Sheets as your database - no coding required.

<Info>
  **Time to complete:** 5 minutes\
  **Prerequisites:** A Google account with access to Google Sheets
</Info>

**What you'll build**

- A fully functional CRM app connected to Google Sheets
- A customized interface with your branding
- A shareable link for your team

## How to build a CRM with Google Sheets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to Google Sheets">
    Open [Zite](https://zite.com) and describe your app idea in the chat. For this example, type:

    > "Build a CRM dashboard connected to Google Sheets"

    <img
      src="/images/Quickstart-CRM1.png"
      alt="Choose a form type"
      style={{ width:"78%" }}
    />

    When prompted, click `Connect` to link your Google account.

    This allows Zite to:

    - Read and write data to your sheets
    - Create new sheets if needed
    - Sync data in real-time

    <img
      src="/images/Quickstart-CRM2.png"
      alt="Choose a form type"
      style={{ width:"62%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select or Create Your Data Source">
    Choose to set up with an **existing sheet** from your Google Drive or let Zite **create new sheet**.

    ![Choose a form type](/images/Quickstart-CRM4.png)

    <Check>
      When creating a new sheet, Zite will automatically set up columns for common CRM fields like Name, Email, Company, Status, etc.
    </Check>
    Once the app is connected to Google Sheets, click `Save` in the popup or `Continue` in the chat to begin building your CRM app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your design">
    Click `Edit` to personalize your app with:

    - **Branding:** Add your logo and company colors
    - **Layout:** Rearrange components and sections
    - **Typography:** Choose fonts that match your brand
    - **Fields:** Add or remove data fields as needed

    Click `Save` when you're satisfied with the design.

    ![Choose a form type](/images/Quickstart-CRM7.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add User Authentication">
    Click **\+** or type:

    > "Add user login"

    ![Screenshot 2025 12 17 At 3 37 17 PM](/images/Screenshot2025-12-17at3.37.17PM.png)

    Click `Edit` on the top right to [customize your login page](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/add-user-login#customize-login-page).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure access control">
    Naviate to `Login` on the top menu and choose who can **Sign up**.

    ![Screenshot 2025 12 17 At 3 46 28 PM](/images/Screenshot2025-12-17at3.46.28PM.png)

    For our CRM app, we only want zite.com company members to be able to access the CRM. Select `Only allowed domains`, add your domain, and then click `Save`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy and share">
    Click `Share` on the upper right corner to get your live app URL. You can [send the link](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/sharing) or [embed](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/embedding) it in your website or company's internal tools.

    That's it! Your app is now live and syncing with Google Sheets in real-time!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Use Cases

Beyond CRM, you can build various apps on Google Sheets:

- **Inventory Management:** Track stock levels and orders
- **Project Trackers:** Manage tasks and deadlines
- **Employee Directories:** Maintain team information
- **Event Registration:** Collect attendee information
- **Budget Planners:** Monitor expenses and revenue
