## How to integrate Fillout

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your form">
    Click **apps** at the bottom left and choose `Fillout`. Then, select the form you want to use in the dropdown menu.

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-17 at 3.05.31 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-17at3.05.31PM.png)

    \
    \
    **or Type a prompt**

    In the chat box, request for your Zite app or for a specific element to be connected with Fillout. You can do this for new and existing apps.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-17at3.23.05PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-17 at 3.23.05 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-17 at 3.23.05 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"60%" }}
    />

    <Warning>
      For security reasons, only forms within the same workspace can be selected.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose embed type">
    Zite automatically chooses a **Default** embed type depending on the look and intended function of your app. To change this, click `Default`.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-17at3.07.37PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-17 at 3.07.37 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-17 at 3.07.37 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"42%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Input embed location">
    In the chat box, indicate where you want the form to show up.

    That's it! Form submissions collected from your Zite will be recorded in the results page of your Fillout page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy and share">
    Use the chat box or the respective buttons in your app to ensure everything's working properly. In our example, we'll "Submit" a general inquiry.

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 4.57.55 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-24at4.57.55PM.png)

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 4.57.01 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-24at4.57.01PM.png)

    Click `Publish` in the upper right corner to [**share**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/sharing) or [**embed**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/embedding) your app.

    [Modify the theme](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/design) or [style specific elements](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/selective-edits) your app to personalize it or match it with your brand.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common use cases

By connecting Fillout, you can easily build apps for things like:

- **Contact Collection:** Capture lead or customer information
- **Calendar Bookings:** Let users schedule appointments directly
- **Feedback Submission:** Quickly gather user thoughts and suggestions
- **Ratings & Reviews:** Prompt users for quick star ratings or testimonials
- **Custom Quizzes:** Deliver interactive assessments or forms with scoring
- **Order Forms:** Collect product or service orders with payment options
- **Incident Reporting:** Enable quick reporting of bugs, issues, or safety concerns
