## Restore older version

<Steps>
  <Step title="View version history" stepNumber={1}>
    Click `History` to open the list of all changes made to your app.

    ![Screenshot 2025-09-09 at 7.08.48 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-09-09at7.08.48PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Undo recent changes">
    In the left sidebar, select the earlier version you prefer. Then, click `Restore`.

    <Warning>
      Restoring the previous version can’t be undone.
    </Warning>
    ![Screenshot 2025-09-09 at 7.09.28 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-09-09at7.09.28PM.png)

    <Tip>
      You can undo a single change (instead of the whole version) via chat. Learn more [here](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/selective-edits#undo-changes).
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy and share">
    Click `Publish` in the upper right corner to [**share**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/sharing) or [**embed**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/embedding) your app.
  </Step>
</Steps>
