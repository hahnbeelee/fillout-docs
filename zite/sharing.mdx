## Share the link

After publishing your app, click `Share` in the upper right corner followed by `Copy`.

![Screenshot 2025-08-20 at 4.33.21 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-20at4.33.21PM.png)

To open your app in a new tab, click the `share icon` beside copy.

## Share the QR code

Choose `QR code` in the **Share** menu, then `Copy` or `Download` the generated code.
