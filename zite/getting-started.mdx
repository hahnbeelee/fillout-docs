### Build your first Zite

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Describe your App**

    [Open Zite](https://zite.com/) and enter the following prompt to start creating your app:

    ```
    I want to build an app that tracks customer feedback with a database.
    ```

    ![Zite app creation prompt](/images/QS1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Connect to the Database**

    Zite will prompt you to connect to a database to store feedback entries. Click `+ Connect`.

    <img
      src="/images/QS2copy.png"
      alt="QS2 copy.png"
      title="QS2 copy.png"
      style={{ width:"66%" }}
    />

    Let's choose to `Create new database`.

    ![Create new database option](/images/QS3.png)

    Zite will create a database for you. Review the fields and click `Create` to proceed.

    ![Database creation confirmation](/images/QS5.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Preview your app**

    Zite provides an interactive version of your feedback tracker, featuring a table to view responses and a form to add feedback.

    ![Screenshot 2025-12-16 at 11.47.41 AM copy.png](/images/Screenshot2025-12-16at11.47.41AMcopy.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Customize the App**

    Click `Edit` to modify the background, text, or other user interface elements. Once satisfied, click `Save`.

    ![Screenshot 2025-12-16 at 11.42.55 AM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-12-16at11.42.55AM.png)

    Check out these [other ways to style your app](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/design).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Share your App**

    Click `Share` followed by `Publish` to generate a live link for your team, customers, or users.

    ![Screenshot 2025-12-16 at 11.58.00 AM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-12-16at11.58.00AM.png)

    Then, [send the link or QR code](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/sharing), or [embed](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/embedding) your Zite app.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-12-16at12.02.01PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-12-16 at 12.02.01 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-12-16 at 12.02.01 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"47%" }}
    />

  <Check>
  In **less than 5 minutes**, you've successfully built a live feedback tracker with a form and a database in just a few steps.
</Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
