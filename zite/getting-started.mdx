### Build your first Zite

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Describe Your App**

    [Open Zite](https://zite.com/) and enter the following prompt to start creating your app:

    ```
    I want to build an app that tracks customer feedback with a database.
    ```

    <Frame>
      ![Zite app creation prompt](/images/QS1.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Connect to the Database**

    Zite will prompt you to connect to a database to store feedback entries. Click **Connect**.

    <Frame>
      ![Database connection prompt](/images/QS2.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Create or Select a Database**

    Click **Create new database** to set up a new database.

    <Frame>
      ![Create new database option](/images/QS3.png)
    </Frame>
    Zite will create a database for you. Review the fields and click **Create** to proceed.

    <Frame>
      ![Database creation confirmation](/images/QS5.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Preview Your App**

    Zite provides an interactive version of your feedback tracker, featuring a table to view responses and a form to enter new feedback.

    <Frame>
      ![App preview with feedback form](/images/QS6.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Customize the App**

    Click **Edit** to modify the background, text, or other user interface elements. Once satisfied, click **Save**.

    <Frame>
      ![App customization options](/images/QS8.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Share Your App**

    Click **Share**.

    <Frame>
      ![App sharing options](/images/QS9.png)
    </Frame>
    Finally, click **Publish** to generate a live link for your team, customers, or users.

    <Frame>
      ![App publishing confirmation](/images/QS10.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  You've successfully built a live feedback tracker with a form and a database in just a few steps.
</Check>

In **less than 5 minutes**, you've built and customized a customer feedback app to share.
