## Available models

### Zite Mini

The **fastest and most cost-efficient** model. Great for quick, simple tasks like minor text changes, small style adjustments, and straightforward edits.

*Powered by Anthropic's Claude Haiku 4.5*

### Zite Pro

The **default model** that offers the best balance of speed and intelligence. Recommended for most tasks, including adding features, fixing bugs, and updating designs.

*Powered by Anthropic's Claude Sonnet 4.5*

### Zite Max

The **most capable model** for maximum intelligence. Use Zite Max for complex work, multi-step reasoning, and persistent issues that other models struggle with.

*Powered by Anthropic's Claude Opus 4.5*

<Info>
  Zite Max is available on the **Business**, **Team**, and **Enterprise** plans.
</Info>

## How to switch models

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the model selector">
    In the chat input toolbar, click the **brain icon** (üß†) to open the model selector dropdown.

    Alternatively, press `Cmd` + `/` (Mac) or `Ctrl` + `/` (Windows/Linux) to toggle the model selector.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your model">
    Select **Zite Mini**, **Zite Pro**, or **Zite Max** depending on your needs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Your model preference is saved automatically on your device.
</Tip>

## Mode-based model behavior

Certain editor modes automatically use **Zite Max** regardless of your selected model:

| Mode           | Model used             |
| -------------- | ---------------------- |
| **Build mode** | Your selected model    |
| **Plan mode**  | Always uses Zite Max   |
| **Chat mode**  | Always uses Zite Max   |

This ensures that planning and conversational tasks benefit from the highest level of reasoning.

## Credit consumption

Different models consume credits at different rates based on their underlying AI costs:

- **Zite Mini**: Most cost-efficient (lowest credit usage per message)
- **Zite Pro**: Moderate credit usage (balanced)
- **Zite Max**: Higher credit usage (premium intelligence)

For more details on credits and usage, check out the [Credits guide](/zite/credits).
