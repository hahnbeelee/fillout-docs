## Customize link preview

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open share menu">
    Click `Share` followed by `Link settings`.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-08-20at6.06.34PMcopy.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-08-20 at 6.06.34 PM copy.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-08-20 at 6.06.34 PM copy.png"
      style={{ width:"43%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit preview info">
    When your app is published, AI generates a default **Title** and **Description**.  You can edit these here.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-08-20at6.17.17PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-08-20 at 6.17.17 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-08-20 at 6.17.17 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"53%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a Thumbnail (Optional)">
    You can `Upload thumbnail` to customize how your app link appears when shared.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a Favicon (Optional)">
    You can also add a favicon, which is the icon that appears in a browser tab like the black image seen below. This is usually a company's logo or icon.

    <img
