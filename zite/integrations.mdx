## Integrate from scratch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an App">
    Click `App` or `+ Create`on your dashboard.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at5.48.05PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 5.48.05 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 5.48.05 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"59%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your integration">
    Enter a prompt for your app, then choose an **app** to integrate it with.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at5.48.58PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 5.48.58 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 5.48.58 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"84%" }}
    />

    Connect to your app. In this example, we'll connect to Google Sheets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select or make a file">
    `Use existing` file

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at5.55.07PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 5.55.07 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 5.55.07 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"48%" }}
    />

    or `create new` ones.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at5.56.08PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 5.56.08 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 5.56.08 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"49%" }}
    />

    It will generate a preview of the file.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at5.56.29PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 5.56.29 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 5.56.29 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy and share">
    Click `Publish` in the upper right corner to [**share**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/sharing) or [**embed**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/embedding) your app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Integrate an existing Zite

Open your Zite and click **apps** at the bottom left. Then, choose your **integration** and follow steps 3 and 4 above.
