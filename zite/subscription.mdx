## Upgrade or downgrade your plan

Directly access your subscription details [here](https://build.fillout.com/home/settings/billing). `Browse plans` for your **Apps** and choose your preferred plan.

![Screenshot 2025-11-14 at 5.33.57 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-14at5.33.57PM.png)

<Warning>
  Only admins can modify a subscription plan.
</Warning>

Alternatively, go to your dashboard. Click your `Account name` on the upper left corner followed by `Settings`. Then, click `Billing & usage` in left side.

## Cancel plan

You can [cancel your Zite app plan](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/cancel-account), and still keep your Fillout Forms plan. To cancel your Zite plan, **downgrade to the Free App plan**.

## The Team plan

If you are a frequent user of Fillout forms, Zite apps, and databases, we recommend checking out our [Team plan](https://www.fillout.com/help/team-plan).
