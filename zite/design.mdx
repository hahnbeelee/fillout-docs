You can style your app by using AI with a reference image, selecting a theme, or starting with a template and editing from there.

## Enter a prompt

In the chat box, drag and drop an image or click + followed by `Upload an attachment`. In your prompt, say whether you want the full design to match the image or just certain parts like fonts or colors.

![Screenshot 2025-11-17 at 5.15.54 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-17at5.15.54PM.png)

<Tip>
  You can make more precise edits with our manual edit tool. Check out our quick guide [here](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/selective-edits).
</Tip>

## Choose a theme

Click the <Icon icon="swatchbook" iconType="solid" /> `paintbrush icon`, then choose your preferences.
