## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the AI Form Maker">
    On Fillout, scroll to the bottom of the page, and click [**AI Survey Maker**](https://www.fillout.com/ai-survey-maker).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose how you want to create your survey">
    Enter the type of survey you need, add your questions, or start from an existing one.

    **Describe survey**
    Type your survey description or use a sample, then click `Generate survey`.

    ![Screenshot 2025-07-18 at 6.23.25 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-07-18at6.23.25PM.png)

    **Import questions**
    List your questions vertically — one line equals one question or field. At the bottom, select how you want the **Questions per page** to be displayed.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-07-18at6.25.26PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-07-18 at 6.25.26 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-07-18 at 6.25.26 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />

    **Import survey**
    You can use surveys you've already created outside Fillout. Choose between these survey builders or enter a survey URL.

    ![Screenshot 2025-07-18 at 6.26.02 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-07-18at6.26.02PM.png)

    <Check>
      **Good to know:** You can bulk import forms, including their responses.
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your AI survey">
    [Style](/themes) your survey, require and [reorder questions](/reorder-with-drag-and-drop), add [logic](/logic), and more. Then, click `Publish` in the upper right corner to share your form via link or embed.

    ![Screenshot 2025-07-18 at 6.27.43 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-07-18at6.27.43PM.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
