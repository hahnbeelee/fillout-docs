## How to set up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an event email reminder">
    Go to **Notifications** on the left-hand panel. Toggle the `Email reminder` switch. Input the **time before event** when you want the reminder to be sent. Alternatively, you can use our `Event email reminder to invitees` [workflow template](/scheduling-workflows#b4ec6e368aca4853afa82cb14846d0cd).

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/scheduling/image-35.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Style your email">
    Click `Edit email` to format the content of your email. Choose a **Style** and a **Theme**.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/scheduling/image-45.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reference fields (optional)">
    You can reference scheduling details or other form answers by typing `@` or clicking the `+` icon, and choosing the specific **field** or **question**.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/scheduling/image-36.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
