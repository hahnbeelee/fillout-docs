## Leave a comment

Open your form, and go to the `Results` page. Right-click a response you’d like to leave a feedback or question on followed by `Add comment`.

![Screenshot 2025-10-09 at 5.20.24 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-09at5.20.24PM.png)

Alternatively, you can hover over the second column from the left and click <Icon icon="arrow-up-right-and-arrow-down-left-from-center" size={15} />.

To `Edit` or `Delete` a comment, click <Icon icon="ellipsis" />.

<img
  src="/images/account/image-26.png"
  alt="Leave a comment"
  style={{ width:"43%" }}
/>

## Mention a team member

In the comment box, tag a user by typing **@**, then select a [team member](/invite-collaborators) in the dropdown.

<img
  src="/images/account/image-27.png"
  alt="Leave a comment"
  style={{ width:"52%" }}
/>

That’s it! The user you tagged will now receive a notification via email.

## Unsubscribe to comments

In the **Comments** panel, simply click `Unsubscribe`.

<img
  src="/images/account/image-28.png"
  alt="Leave a comment"
  style={{ width:"42%" }}
/>
