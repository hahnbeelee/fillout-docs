## How to add an address field

Let respondents enter their physical mailing address with separate input boxes for the street address, city, state/province, and postal code. Simply drag the `Address` field from the left-hand panel.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/question-types/image-1.png)
</Frame>

Optionally allow users to choose a **Country**. Click the `settings icon` to open the right-hand panel and turn off the `Fixed country` switch.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/question-types/image-2.png)
</Frame>

## Use Google Maps Autocomplete

Increase conversion rates and address accuracy by enabling Google Maps Autocomplete. Click the address field followed by the `settings icon` and toggle the `Address autocomplete` switch in the right-hand panel at the bottom.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/question-types/image-3.gif)
</Frame>

<Note>
  **Note:** Google Maps Autocomplete is currently available on the **Enterprise** plan. For more details on pricing, click [**here**](https://www.fillout.com/pricing). Contact [support@fillout.com](mailto:support@fillout.com) for details.
</Note>
