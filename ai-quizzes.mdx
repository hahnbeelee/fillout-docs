## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the AI Quiz Maker">
    On Fillout, scroll to the bottom of the page, and click [**AI Quiz Maker**](https://www.fillout.com/ai-quiz-maker).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a description">
    Describe the kind of quiz you want, then choose a **Difficulty** and a theme **Design** to `Generate quiz`.

    ![Screenshot 2025-07-18 at 6.45.13 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-07-18at6.45.13PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your AI quiz">
    [Style](/themes) your quiz, set up [scoring](https://www.fillout.com/help/make-a-free-quiz#how-to-create-a-quiz-in-fillout), and [choose which ending page to display](https://www.fillout.com/help/create-a-lead-quiz#add-calculations-to-ending-pages-and-custom-emails) depending on the outcome.

    ![Screenshot 2025-07-18 at 6.46.27 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-07-18at6.46.27PM.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Use OpenAI Quiz GPT

Build smarter quizzes faster by adding the AI Quiz Maker extension to ChatGPT. Click `Try the Open AI Quiz GPT` [here](https://www.fillout.com/ai-quiz-maker).
