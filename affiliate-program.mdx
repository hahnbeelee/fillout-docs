## How to join

Create a [Fillout account](https://build.fillout.com/signup?ref=fillouthelparticle-affiliateprogram), if you haven't already. Next, visit our [**Affiliate program page**](https://www.fillout.com/affiliate-program?ref=fillout-help-article-affiliate), and click `Start earning today`.


In the popup window, click `Become an affiliate`. Then, enter your preferred referral identifier.

## How it works

Affiliate links track referrals and earn you commissions when new users sign up for Fillout through your unique URLs.

- **30% recurring commission** for up to one year
- Only works for **new users** (existing users can't be referred)

## Managing your links

In your affiliate dashboard, you can:

- **View your main affiliate link** - Your default URL for general use
- **Create custom links** - Create multiple links for different campaigns
- **View real-time analytics** - Track clicks, sign-ups, and earnings

## Common questions

**Q: How do I get paid?**

Commissions are paid monthly via your preferred payment ACH method set in the affiliate dashboard.

**Q: Can I customize my link?**

Yes, you can customize the handle/slug in your dashboard.

**Q: What if someone clicks multiple affiliate links?**

The first affiliate link clicked gets credit for the conversion.

**Q: What if I forgot to use my affiliate link for a client signup?**

Currently we can only apply affiliate commissions retroactively for verified solution partners. Apply to become a solution partner [here](https://main.fillout.com/solution-partner-application).
