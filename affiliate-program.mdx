## How to join

Create a [Fillout account](https://build.fillout.com/signup?ref=fillouthelparticle-affiliateprogram), if you haven't already. Next, visit our [**Affiliate program page**](https://www.fillout.com/affiliate-program?ref=fillout-help-article-affiliate), and click `Start earning today`.

![How to join](/images/account/image-19.png)

In the popup window, click `Become an affiliate`. Then, enter your preferred referral identifier.

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-17at4.25.20PM.png"
  alt="Screenshot 2025-10-17 at 4.25.20 PM.png"
  title="Screenshot 2025-10-17 at 4.25.20 PM.png"
  style={{ width:"75%" }}
/>

<Check>
  Your referrals will also get extra [Zite](zite.com?ref=fillout-help-article-affiliate) credits upon signup.
</Check>

That's it! Now you'll be able share your **unique referral link** via messaging, email, socials, and more. You can check your **affiliate dashboard** to see how many users subscribed or upgraded to a paid plan through your referral.
