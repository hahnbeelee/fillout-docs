## Upgrade or downgrade your plan

You can directly access billing [here](https://app.fillout.com/home/settings/billing).

![Screenshot 2025-11-14 at 6.08.04â€¯PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-14at6.08.04PM.png)

Alternatively, click your `Account name` on the upper left corner followed by `Settings`. Then, click `Billing & Usage` in left the side to `Browse plans`.

## Cancel Fillout plan

You can cancel your Fillout forms plan, and still keep your Zite app plan. Simply **downgrade to a Free Forms plan**.

## Get invoices

You can access prior billing invoices in the [billing portal](https://app.fillout.com/home/settings/billing). Click on an invoice to download it.

![Get invoices](/images/account/image-4.png)

## Cancel your subscription

You can also cancel your subscription in the [billing portal](https://app.fillout.com/home/settings/billing). Click `Change plan` and choose the **Free** tier.
