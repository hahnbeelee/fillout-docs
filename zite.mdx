## Why Choose Zite

**Built by Chat**

Simply type what you need—Zite builds it for you, instantly.

**Advanced features**

Use chat to add a full-featured database and user login.

**Connects with Your Tools**

Integrate with Google Sheets, Airtable, Fillout, Slack, and more. Pull in live data, send alerts, and automate workflows.

**Launch Fast**

Transform your idea into a live app in minutes. Zite handles design, structure, and backend, allowing you to focus on creating your app.
