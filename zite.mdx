### What kind of apps can I build?

You can build all kinds of apps that connect to your data. 

Common examples include:

1. CRM app connected to Airbase for data storage
2. Client Portal connected to Google Sheets for data storage
3. Employee Intranet connected to Slack which notifies you when someone updates the intranet
4. A website with a Fillout form embedded that will notify you when someone complets the form
