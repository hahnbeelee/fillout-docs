## How to build a form in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up or log in">
    Sign up [here](https://app.fillout.com/signup) or log in [here](https://app.fillout.com/login) using your Google account, Microsoft account, or any email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a form type">
    Click `+ Create` or `Form`, then choose between a **Blank form**, **Template**, or **Connect** with 3rd party apps like Airtable, Notion, Hubspot, and more.

    <img
      src="/images/Maf2.png"
      alt="Maf 2.png"
      title="Maf 2.png"
      style={{ width:"52%" }}
      className="mr-auto"
    />

    If you’re unsure about which to use, choose `Blank form`. You can always connect to an [integration](/integrations) later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize the design">
    Click `Theme` to pick from one of the pre-made themes or [customize your own](/themes).

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-01 at 4.17.28 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at4.17.28PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add questions or fields">
    Click or drag and drop your desired [field types](/question-types) from the left-hand panel. Choose between over 50 different types.

    ![Maf 4 1001.png](/images/Maf41001.png)

    Then, enter the question or text. Click it's <Icon icon="gear" iconType="solid" /> icon to customize it in the right-hand panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish and share">
    `Preview` your form to test it. Once everything's good to go, press `Publish`.

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-01 at 4.12.05 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at4.12.05PM.png)

    That’s it! Share your **form link** anywhere or **embed your form** on any website. To view form submissions, head over to [**Results**](/view-responses) in the top menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>
