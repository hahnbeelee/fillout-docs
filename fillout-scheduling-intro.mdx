## How to set up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your calendar">
    Go to `Scheduling` settings by clicking <Icon icon="gear-complex" iconType="solid" />.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-20at5.54.06PMcopy.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-20 at 5.54.06 PM copy.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-20 at 5.54.06 PM copy.png"
      style={{ width:"45%" }}
    />

    In the popup, go to `Connected Calendars` and add your Google or Outlook calendar.

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-20 at 5.55.18 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-20at5.55.18PM.png)

    <Check>
      Fillout Scheduling syncs availability across all connected calendars.
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a scheduling form">
    Go back to `Home` and click `Form`. Alternatively, you may click `Create` followed by `Scheduling`.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-21at2.01.51PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-21 at 2.01.51 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-21 at 2.01.51 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"68%" }}
    />

    Choose `Scheduling`followed by your preferred [meeting type](/event-types).

    <img
      src="/images/scheduling/image-19.png"
      alt=""
      style={{ width:"94%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure event details">
    In the left-hand panel, add your meeting details, and optionally **\+ Add meeting location** to include a physical location, phone number, or a Google Meet or Zoom link for the meeting.

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-20 at 6.04.06 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-20at6.04.06PM.png)

    Fillout creates a scheduling form with default hours, Monday to Friday, 9 AM–5 PM. Customize it via [Availability](https://www.fillout.com/help/fillout-scheduling-availability#set-up-via-form-editor) in the left hand-panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize the style">
    Go to [Theme](/customize-fillout-scheduling) to edit your form's design. You even [change your avatar](https://www.fillout.com/help/customize-fillout-scheduling#change-your-avatar) to your logo to match your brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish and share">
    That’s it! `Publish` your form and share the link or embed it anywhere.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Fillout Scheduling Works

[Fillout Scheduling](https://www.fillout.com/scheduling) forms let people book available slots on your calendar while ensuring the time works for you. The forms show a calendar, meeting details, and organizer info by default.

![Screenshot 2025-10-20 at 6.12.29 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-20at6.12.29PM.png)

<Check>
  Respondents can select their timezone to view slots in local time.
</Check>

Fillout will prompt for their name and email. After clicking `Schedule event`, respondents will receive an email containing the details and a reschedule link. Events are automatically added to Google and Outlook calendars.

![Screenshot 2025-10-20 at 6.20.22 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-20at6.20.22PM.png)
