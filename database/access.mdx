## Share your database

By default, other workspace users can access your database. Click `Share` on the top right to `Edit` **People with access**.

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2025-12-11at6.42.03PM.png"

  title="Screenshot 2025-12-11 at 6.42.03 PM.png"
  style={{ width:"53%" }}
/>

Click `Add people` to invite others.

You can also make your database accessible to `Anyone with the link`.
