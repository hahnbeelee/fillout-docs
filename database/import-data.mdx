## Add data to a new database

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose database">
    From the dashboard, click `Database`. Alternatively, you may click `Create` on the upper left corner.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-11-20at4.34.41PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 4.34.41 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 4.34.41 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"85%" }}
    />

    Choose to `Import` between a CSV, Google Sheets, or Airtable. In this example, we'll choose `Airtable`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an account and base">
    Select your **source account** and a **base** from there.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-11-20at4.50.46PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 4.50.46 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 4.50.46 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"55%" }}
    />

    That's it! You now have a Zite database with external data.

    ![Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 4.55.16 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-20at4.55.16PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add fields (Optional)">
    Click `+ Add field` beside the rightmost column.

    <img
      src="/images/image.png"
      alt="image.png"
      title="image.png"
      style={{ width:"49%" }}
    />

    **Add tables**

    Click <Icon icon="square-plus" /> at the bottom right followed by `New table`.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-11-20at4.55.41PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 4.55.41 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 4.55.41 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"33%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build forms and apps (Optional)">
    Connect your database to Fillout Forms or Zite Apps. Your data syncs automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share (Optional)">
    If necessary, `Share` your database to other users using the option on the top right corner.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add data to an exsiting database

### Import to a new table

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to your database">
    Open your Zite app and click `Database` on the top menu.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at3.51.50PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.51.50 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.51.50 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"82%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an external source">
    Click **\+** followed by your **database source**.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at3.03.01PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.03.01 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.03.01 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"35%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your database">
    Upload your CSV file

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at3.21.37PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.21.37 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.21.37 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"46%" }}
    />

    or choose an existing database from Google Sheets or Airtable.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at3.17.40PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.17.40 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.17.40 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"59%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and edit">
    View a sample of your data and change field types before importing by clicking the **column header**. When everything's all set, `Import database`.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at3.07.15PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.07.15 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.07.15 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"83%" }}
    />

    That's it! A new table will be added to your database.

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.09.02 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at3.09.02PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Go back to your `App` and click `Publish` in the upper right corner.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Import to an existing table

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to your database">
    Open your Zite app and click `Database` on the top menu.

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.20.48 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at3.20.48PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an external source">
    Click <Icon icon="ellipsis" /> next to the name of your table followed by `Import data` and select your **database source**.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at3.20.58PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.20.58 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.20.58 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"48%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your database">
    Upload your CSV file

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at3.21.37PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.21.37 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.21.37 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"46%" }}
    />

    or choose an existing database from Google Sheets or Airtable.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at3.17.40PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.17.40 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.17.40 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"59%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and edit">
    You'll see a preview of the columns to be imported. Here, you can `Edit mapping` to change which columns on your external file you'd like to match with columns on your existing Zite database. `Import database` when the mapping's all set.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at3.24.36PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.24.36 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.24.36 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"82%" }}
    />

    That's it! New rows have now be added to your existing table.

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.25.58 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at3.25.58PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh and publish">
    Go back to your `App` and click <Icon icon="arrows-rotate" />. Changes will now be reflected to your app. Finally, click `Publish` in the upper right corner.

    ![Screenshot 2025-10-03 at 3.48.50 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-03at3.48.50PM.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
