<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit ChatGPT in your browser">
    Go to [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com/)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Settings -> Apps">
    Click on your avatar in the lower left to open the menu and then choose Settings, and then select the Apps tab.

    ![chatgpt-apps.png](/images/mcp/chatgpt-apps.png)

  </Step>
   <Step title="Turn on developer mode">
    In order to add a custom connector in GPT you'll have to turn on developer mode. Click on advanced settings, and then select the
    toggle for developer mode to enable it.

    ![chatgpt-developer-mode.png](/images/mcp/chatgpt-developer-mode.png)

    Then, click back to return to the Apps settings tab.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a custom app">
    Click the "Create app" button and enter the following details:

    * Name: Zite
    * MCP Server URL: https://mcp.fillout.com/mcp
    * Authentication: OAuth

    Select the checkbox to indicate that you understand the risk from custom MCP apps, and then click the "Create" button to
    create the app.

    ![chatgpt-new-app.png](/images/mcp/chatgpt-new-app.png)

  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize the connection">
    GPT will send you over to Zite with this authorization screen:

    ![chatgpt-oauth.png](/images/mcp/chatgpt-oauth.png)

    Click the "Authorize" button to complete your connection. This will send you back to ChatGPT's settings page with the connector
    fully set up. You should see a green popup indicating that the app has been added successfully.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting">
    You're all set. At this point you can head back to ChatGPT's chat interface and start accessing your Zite data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
