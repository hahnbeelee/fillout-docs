## Build from scratch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose blank database">
    From the dashboard, click `Database` followed by [Blank database](http://build.fillout.com/home/database/new) in the popup.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-11-20at4.34.41PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 4.34.41 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 4.34.41 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"78%" }}
    />

    Alternatively, you may click `+ Create` on the upper left corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add and edit fields">
    Click `+ Add field` beside the rightmost column and use 20+ field types including text, numbers, dates, files, linked records, and more.

    ![Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 4.48.47 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-21at4.48.47PM.png)

    **Add tables**

    Optionally add multiple tables to organize your data. On the bottom left, click <Icon icon="square-plus" /> beside **Table**.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-11-21at4.47.00PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 4.47.00 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 4.47.00 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"40%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build forms and apps">
    Connect your database to Fillout Forms or Zite Apps through the top menu. Your data syncs automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share (Optional)">
    Click `Share` in the top right to collaborate with your team. Inviting users is free!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Use existing data

You can use data from tools you already use as a starting point. Simply import from a CSV, Google Sheets, or Airtable. Check out [this guide](https://www.fillout.com/help/database/import-data).
