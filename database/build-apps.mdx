## Create an app

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your Zite database">
    Choose a database from the [dashboard](https://build.fillout.com/home).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Apps page">
    On the top of your database, click `Apps` followed by `+ Create`.

    ![Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 7.35.23 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-21at7.35.23PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your App">
    Enter a prompt and be as specific as possible.

    ![Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 7.38.53 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-21at7.38.53PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your App">
    Your app is now ready to be used! Click `Preview` to test it. If needed, ask the AI to make changes using the chat box in the lower-left corner, or use [selective edits](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/selective-edits).

    ![Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 7.57.29 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-21at7.57.29PM.png)

    You can also change the theme or design of your app. Check out [this quick guide](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/design).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy and share">
    Click `Publish` in the upper right corner to [**share**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/sharing) or [**embed**](https://www.fillout.com/help/zite/embedding) your app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect a database to your existing app

If you've already made an app, you can simply connect this to a database.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your Zite app">
    Navigate to your app in the [Zite builder](https://build.fillout.com/home).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect database">
    Click `Database` in the top menu followed by `Connect to database`.

    ![Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 7.16.16 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-21at7.16.16PM.png)

    Select an existing Zite Database or create a new one.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-11-21at6.54.38PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 6.54.38 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 6.54.38 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"56%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and edit">
    You'll see a preview of the tables and columns that it will generate. Here, you can add more tables `+`.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-11-21at7.06.42PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 7.06.42 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 7.06.42 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"80%" }}
    />

    You can also add fields to existing tables `+`. Enter a name on the left and choose the type on the right. For choice fields (e.g., Single select or Multiple select) open field settings <Icon icon="gear-complex" iconType="regular" /> to add the options.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-11-21at7.12.18PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 7.12.18 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 7.12.18 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"81%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    That's it! Your database is now ready to be used. Display records, create forms, or build custom interfaces. `Publish` your app to reflect the recent changes.

    ![Screenshot 2025-11-21 at 7.03.17 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-21at7.03.17PM.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Working with database data

Once connected, you can:

- **Display records**: Show your database records in tables, lists, or cards
- **Create forms**: Build forms that add new records to your database
- **Update records:** Edit existing records through your app interface
- **Filter and search:** Let users find specific records
- **Use relationships:** Display linked records across tables
