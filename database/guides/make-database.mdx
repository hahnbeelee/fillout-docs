## Build from scratch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start from scratch">
    From the dashboard, click `Database`. Choose `Blank database` in the popup.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-11-20at4.34.41PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 4.34.41 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 4.34.41 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"78%" }}
    />

    Alternatively, you may click `Create` on the upper left corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add and edit fields">
    Click `+ Add field` beside the rightmost column and use 20+ field types including text, numbers, dates, files, linked records, and more.

    ![Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 5.40.45 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-20at5.40.45PM.png)

    To change the field type, hover over the column header and click <Icon icon="caret-down" iconType="solid" /> followed by `Edit field`.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-11-20at5.40.19PMcopy.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 5.40.19 PM copy.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 5.40.19 PM copy.png"
      style={{ width:"20%" }}
    />

    **Add tables**

    Optionally add multiple tables to organize your data. Click <Icon icon="square-plus" /> beside **Table**.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-11-20at5.45.25PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 5.45.25 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-11-20 at 5.45.25 PM.png"
      style={{ width:"41%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build forms and apps (Optional)">
    Connect your database to Fillout Forms or Zite Apps through the top menu. Your data syncs automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share (Optional)">
    If necessary, `Share` your database to other users by clicking the option on the top right.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Use existing data

You can use data from tools you already use as a starting point. Simply import from a CSV, Google Sheets, or Airtable. Check out this guide.
