## Link your form to a Zite database

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Zite Database">
    Open your form and click the `Integrate` tab at the top. Select **Zite Database** from the list of integrations.

    ![Screenshot 2025-12-11 at 5.52.25 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-12-11at5.52.25PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose database option">
    Pick **New database** to create a fresh database for this form, or use an **Existing database**.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2026-01-07at4.59.59PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2026 01 07 At 4 59 59 PM"
      title="Screenshot 2026 01 07 At 4 59 59 PM"
      style={{ width:"62%" }}
    />

    If you choose an **existing database**, a new table will be created specifically for this form's responses.

    <img
      src="/images/linktodbsuccess.png"
      alt="linktodbsuccess.png"
      style={{ width:"68%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="View your data">
    Your form is now linked to a database! You can view responses in two places:

    - **From your form**: Click the [Results](https://www.fillout.com/help/results) tab at the top where you can update or [edit responses](https://www.fillout.com/help/edit-submissions#edit-form-submissions).
    - **From the database**: Navigate to your database and click the `Forms` tab to see all linked forms

    ![zitedbtabs.png](/images/zitedbtabs.png)

    New responses will sync automatically to your database in real-time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Forms page">
    On the top of your database, click `Forms` followed by `+ Create`.

    ![Screenshot 2025-12-11 at 5.44.17 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-12-11at5.44.17PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make a new form">
    Choose to `Create` a **new form**.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-12-11at6.02.11PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot 2025-12-11 at 6.02.11 PM.png"
      title="Screenshot 2025-12-11 at 6.02.11 PM.png"

## What happens next

Once linked, every form submission creates a new record in your database. You can:

- View and edit responses directly in the database
- Add custom fields to store additional data
- [Build apps](https://www.fillout.com/help/database/build-apps#create-an-app) on top of your form data
- Use the REST API to access your responses programmatically

<Check>
  Form responses sync instantly to your database. Any changes you make in the database won't affect the original form submission.
</Check>
