## How to add a video

First, drag the `Video` field onto your form.

Click <Icon icon="gear" iconType="solid" /> to open the right-hand panel. Paste the **Video URL** of the video you want to display. Adjust the **Max height** as needed.

## Let respondents upload a video

To collect video submissions from your respondents, simply click the `File uploader` field in the left-hand panel. Check out our quick guide [here](/file-upload).
