## How to view submissions

Open your form, and click `Results` on  the top menu. Sort responses or hide columns by hovering over a column header, followed by clicking <Icon icon="caret-down" iconType="solid" color="#000000" size={18} />.

![Screenshot 2026 01 07 At 5 13 48 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-01-07at5.13.48PM.png)

###  Filter submissions

`Filter` to narrow responses based on a specific preference. Click `All time` to change the date range you're viewing.

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2026-01-07at5.21.48PM.png"
  alt="Screenshot 2026 01 07 At 5 21 48 PM"
  title="Screenshot 2026 01 07 At 5 21 48 PM"
  style={{ width:"88%" }}
/>

###  Export submissions

Click the download icon in the top right to export all of your responses in a formatted CSV respective of the filters.

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2026-01-07at5.23.40PM-1.png"
  alt="Screenshot 2026 01 07 At 5 23 40 PM"
  title="Screenshot 2026 01 07 At 5 23 40 PM"
  style={{ width:"22%" }}
/>

### <Icon icon="keyboard" iconType="solid" color="#000000" size="22" /> Shortcuts

- `⌘ + F` or `Ctrl + F` while clicking on any cell to search through your entries
- `⌘ + C` or `Ctrl + C` on a cell to copy its contents
- `Double-click any cell` to enlarge its contents (helpful for long inputs)

## View individual submissions

Hover over a cell and click  <Icon icon="arrow-up-right-and-arrow-down-left-from-center" size={14} />  to view the answers and submission details. Here, you can [comment and tag](/comment) your teammates.

![Screenshot 2026 01 07 At 5 17 35 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-01-07at5.17.35PM.png)

Click <Icon icon="angle-up" /> or <Icon icon="angle-down" /> to quickly go to view other individual responses as a whole.

## Check misconfiguration errors

Fillout alerts you of any misconfiguration errors. For example, you recently changed a field type in your Airtable/Notion database, but forgot to change it in Fillout.

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2026-01-07at5.33.43PM.png"
  alt="Screenshot 2026 01 07 At 5 33 43 PM"
  title="Screenshot 2026 01 07 At 5 33 43 PM"
  style={{ width:"53%" }}
/>

Click <Icon icon="triangle-exclamation" /> to view the error in the right-hand panel. You can also enable [error notifications](/email-notifications) to get notified via email.
