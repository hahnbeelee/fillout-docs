On your dashboard, click your `Account name` followed by `Settings` in the dropdown menu.

## Change account email

If you want to change the email linked to your account, go to `My account` and update your email.

## Upgrade plan

To upgrade your plan, go to `Billing` and click `Change plan`. More info [here](/billing-and-payments).

## Key features

**Account Upgrade**

Upgrade your account to access advanced Fillout features.

**Workspace Management**

Create and manage [workspaces](/workspaces) to organize your forms and collaborate with others. You can create separate workspaces for different projects or teams.

**Collaboration**

[Invite users](/invite-collaborators) to collaborate on your forms by granting them access to specific workspaces or forms. [Control their permissions](/granular-permissions) and decide whether they can view, edit, [comment](/comment), or manage your forms.
