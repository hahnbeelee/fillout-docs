## Design your form

Click `Theme` to open the form designer, where you can edit the colors, font, and layout of your current [theme](/themes). To change this, go to `All themes`.

![Screenshot 2025-10-21 at 5.22.34 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-21at5.22.34PM.png)

To make [advanced customizations](/advanced-designer), scroll down in the left-hand panel to open the `Advanced designer`.

## Edit form link

In the `Share` page of your form, you can easily customize your link ending or slug. More info [here](/custom-link).

![Screenshot 2025-10-21 at 5.37.50 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-21at5.37.50PM.png)

Paid Fillout accounts can also [add a custom domain](/custom-domains) for fully branded scheduling forms.

## Change your avatar

Simply hover over the avatar in your form to `Customize` it. Alternatively, you may do so in the [booking page](/how-to-create-scheduling-form#1590ee47667b80718a29f72c05c84a8d) from the left-hand panel.
