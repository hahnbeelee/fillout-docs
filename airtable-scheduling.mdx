## Overview

Create a free scheduling form that streamlines appointment booking process and sends all event details directly to `Airtable`. This guide explains how to set up a scheduling form, connect it to Google Calendar and integrate it with your Airtable bases.

## How to set up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a free Scheduling Form">
    [Create a Scheduling Form](https://build.fillout.com/new/scheduling) or add a [scheduling page](/scheduling-page) to an existing Fillout form. You’ll be prompted to connect your Google or Outlook Calendar and optionally connect Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet, as a meeting location.

    ![Screenshot 2025-11-10 at 11.06.34 AM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-10at11.06.34AM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Airtable">
    Click `Integrate` on the top menu and connect to `Airtable`.

    ![Screenshot 2025-11-10 at 11.08.27 AM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-10at11.08.27AM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose where to send booking details">
    You can map the following fields, directly to Airtable:

    <div className="flex flex-row gap-8">
    <div>
    - Invitee Email
    - Event Start Time
    - Event End Time
    - Event URL

    </div>

    <div>
    - Full name
    - Meeting notes
    - Phone (if provided)
    - Google Meet/Zoom Link

    </div>

    </div>

    ![Screenshot 2025-11-10 at 11.12.08 AM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-10at11.12.08AM.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
