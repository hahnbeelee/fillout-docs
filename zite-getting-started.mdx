## Build Your First Zite

Go from idea to live app in just a few minutes. Whether itâ€™s a CRM, client portal, employee onboarding or any other type of app Zite makes it easy to build and share without writing a single line of code. 

Follow the steps below to create and launch your first app to track customer feedback.

<Steps>

<Step>
**Describe Your App**

Open Zite and type the following prompt:

```
I want to build an app that tracks customer feedback with a database.
```

<Frame>
  ![Choose a form type](/images/QS1.png)
</Frame>

Zite will start creating your app immediately.



</Step>

<Step>
**Connect to the database**

Zite will prompt you to Connect to a database to store feedback entries. Click **Connect**.

<Frame>
  ![Choose a form type](/images/QS2.png)
</Frame>

</Step>

<Step>

**Create a new database or select an existing database**

Click Create new database.

<Frame>
  ![Choose a form type](/images/QS3.png)
</Frame>

Next click **Create**
<Frame>
  ![Choose a form type](/images/QS4.png)
</Frame>

</Step>

<Step>

**Preview Your App**

Zite provides a working version of your feedback tracker, featuring a form and a table to view responses.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of previewed feedback app in Zite](#)
</Frame>

</Step>

<Step>

**Customize the App**

Click **Edit** to modify form fields, app title, or colors. Once satisfied with the changes, click **Save**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of editing form fields in Zite](#)
</Frame>

</Step>

<Step>

**Share Your App**

Click **Share** to generate a live link that you can distribute to your team, customers, or users.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of share modal in Zite](#)
</Frame>

</Step>

</Steps>

<Check> You've successfully built a live feedback tracker with a form, database, and dashboard in just a few steps.</Check>
