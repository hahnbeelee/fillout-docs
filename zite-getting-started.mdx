## Build and Share Your First Zite

Go from idea to live app in just a few minutes by typing. Whether it’s a CRM, Client Portal, Employee onboarding or any other type of app Zite makes it easy to build and share without writing a single line of code. 

Follow the steps below to create and launch your first app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with a Prompt">
    Open Zite and type what you want to build, for this example we will build a CRM dashboard that is connected to Google Sheets.
     <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/auth-step-1.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch Your App Take Shape">
  Zite will begin building your app. No code, no setup required.

    <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/auth-step-2.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Data">
  For this example Zite will prompt us to connect to Google Sheets as data storage for our CRM.

     <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/auth-step-3.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Your App">
  Click Edit to tweak the design and personalize your app.
  <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/auth-step-4.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add User Login (Optional)">
   If you want to control access, type “Add user login” and Zite will generate a login page. You can choose who gets access — anyone, invite-only, or company members.
  <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/auth-step-5.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
   <Step title="Share your app">
   Click Share and your app is now live. You will get a link to share your app.
  <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/auth-step-5.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

In less than 5 minutes we have built and customized a CRM app to share.
