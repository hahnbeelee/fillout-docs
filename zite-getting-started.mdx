## Build and Share Your First Zite

Go from idea to live app in just a few minutes by typing. Whether it’s a CRM, Client Portal, Employee onboarding or any other type of app Zite makes it easy to build and share without writing a single line of code. 

Follow the steps below to create and launch your first app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with a Prompt">
    Open Zite and **type** what you want to build, for this example we will build a **CRM dashboard that is connected to Google Sheets**.
     <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/Quickstart-CRM1.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your app to Google Sheets">
  Zite will prompt you to connect your app to Google Sheets, click **Connect** to proceed.

    <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/Quickstart-CRM2.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use an existing sheet or create a new one to use as your data storage">
  The Google Sheet you select will be used to store and retrieve your CRM data.

     <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/Quickstart-CRM4.png)
    </Frame>
    After the app has connected to Google Sheets click **Continue** in the chat to begin building your CRM app.
    <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/Quickstart-CRM5.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Zite will build your App">
  Zite will now build out your app. Once it is completed you can see a preview.
  <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/Quickstart-CRM6.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Your App">
  Click **Edit** and select the element you want to customize such as background color, adding a logo or the font. Then click **Save**.
  <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/Quickstart-CRM7.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add User Login">
   To control access to our CRM app **type “Add user login”** and Zite will generate a full user login for your app. 
    <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/Quickstart-CRM8.png)
    </Frame>
    Zite will build your user login and show a preview when it is completed. 
    <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/Quickstart-CRM9.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
   <Step title="Edit your login page">
   Click **Edit** to make changes to the login page and click **Save**.

     <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/Quickstart-CRM10.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
   <Step title="Choose who gets access">
   Click **Users -> Settings** and choose who gets access — anyone, invite-only, or company members. 

     <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/Quickstart-CRM12.png)
    </Frame>
    
    For our CRM app we only want zite.com company members to be able to access the CRM, select **Only Allowed Domains** and enter your domain then click **Save**.

  </Step>
   <Step title="Share your app">
   Click **Share** and your app is now live and ready to use. You will get a link to share your app with your team members.
  <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/Quickstart-CRM13.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

In **less than 5 minutes** we have built and customized a CRM app to share. If you have any issues or questions email support@fillout.com.
