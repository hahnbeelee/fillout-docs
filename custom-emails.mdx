## How to send a custom email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with a workflow">
    Click [**Workflows**](/workflows) on the top menu. Then, choose a template under **Notifications**. Alternatively, you may open a form and go to its **Settings** followed by **Custom emails**.

    <Frame>
      ![Fillout Workflow](/images/workflows/image-13.png)
    </Frame>
    Choose if you want to send one right away through a **Thank you email**, **Send email only if conditions match**, or **Send email after delay**. `Create workflow` and select which form you’d like to **apply the workflow to** in the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your email">
    Then, `Edit` the content of your email

    <Frame>
      ![Fillout Workflows](/images/workflows/image-14.png)
    </Frame>
    Edit the **Subject**, add **Attachments**, choose where to **Send email from**, and more. You can also opt to `Send from your Gmail +` account.

    <Frame>
      ![Fillout Workflow](/images/workflows/image-15.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Format email style

<Steps>
  <Step title="Style your email">
    To customize the look of your message, go to `Email`. Select your preferred **Style** and **Theme**. Click the `+` icon to add more **Text**, an **Image**, **Spacer**, and a link **Button**.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2025-06-11 at 7.17.56 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-06-11at7.17.56PM.png)
    </Frame>
    To change the text formatting and color, simply highlight any text.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2025-06-11 at 7.18.50 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-06-11at7.18.50PM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reference answers (optional)">
    You can also pipe answers, like First Name, by typing `@` or clicking the blue `+` icon and choosing the corresponding field or question.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2025-06-11 at 7.19.08 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-06-11at7.19.08PM.png)
    </Frame>
    [Calculations](/calculations) can also be used to insert dynamic text into your emails. Be sure to `Test` your email workflow before clicking `Publish` on the upper right corner.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Send different messages based on responses

<Steps>
  <Step title='Select "Send email only if conditions match" template'>
    You can send a different message to a group of respondents depending on the answers they have provided in the form. Choose **Send email only if conditions match** under **Notifications**. `Create workflow` and `Edit` the **Filter**.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2025-06-11 at 7.13.02 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-06-11at7.13.02PM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your condition">
    Set up conditional logic by choosing a question followed by a condition. For example, we’ll send a custom email to those who responded Yes to Will you be attending the event?

    <Frame>
      ![Fillout Workflow](/images/workflows/image-20.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Send emails after a specified duration

Opt to **Send email after delay** and `Edit` how later you want to send it in minutes, hours, or days.
