## Manage future bookings

Simply toggle the `Limit future bookings` switch to specify how many **days into the future** respondents can reserve time slots in your calendar, or add a **date range**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/scheduling/image-27.png)
</Frame>

## Add minimum notice period

You can prevent last minute bookings by requiring `Minimum notice` and setting your preferred hours.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/scheduling/image-28.png)
</Frame>

## Limit daily events

Prevent overbooking and add a `Daily event limit` in your scheduling form. Enter the maximum number of events you would like to accommodate in a day.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/scheduling/image-44.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  **Note:** This is only applicable to one-on-one events.
</Note>

## Add buffer times

When editing a form, you can toggle the `Buffer Time` switch on the left-hand panel to add break times before and/or after an event. This gives you time to prepare for the next event or wrap up the previous one.
