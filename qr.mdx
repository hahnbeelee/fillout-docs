## How it works

Generating **QR codes** for forms enables quick and easy mobile access to forms, surveys, or other response materials. This feature enhances user engagement and promotes efficient data collection.

## How to share a form QR

After publishing your form, click <Icon icon="qrcode" />. Alternatively, you can click `Share` in the top center or right.
