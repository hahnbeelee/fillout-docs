## How to enable RTL feature

First, click ICON from the top right corner to open form settings.

![Screenshot 2025-10-10 at 5.19.16 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-10at5.19.16PM.png)

Go to `Language` and enable `Right to left`. 

![Screenshot 2025-10-10 at 5.20.34 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-10at5.20.34PM.png)

Remember to `Publish` your form to make the changes reflect.

![Screenshot 2025-10-10 at 5.24.58 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-10-10at5.24.58PM.png)
