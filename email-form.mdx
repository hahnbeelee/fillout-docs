## How to send your form via email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Share page">
    First, go to the **Share** tab from the top menu. Click `Send form`.

    <Frame>
      ![PDF Viewer](/images/guides/image-2.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the contents of your email">
    Click `Set recipients` to enter the **email addresses** of the people whom you would like to send it to. You can also customize the **Message** and [format the style](/custom-emails#c6872760d8cb46d382efc4089b770e9a) of the email.

    <Frame>
      ![PDF Viewer](/images/guides/image-3.png)
    </Frame>
    Here, you can also enable `Reminders` and set your preferred **Frequency**.

    <Check>
      **Good to know:** You can add up to 50 email addresses per hour as recipients in unverified accounts. If you [verify your account](https://main.fillout.com/verify-your-fillout-account), you can add up to 250 email addresses per hour. 
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Track submissions

In the same pop-up window, click `Track responses`. Alternatively, you can go back to the **Share** page to do this. Here, you'll see if a recipient has **Not started**, is **In progress**, or has **Completed** your form.

  ![PDF Viewer](/images/guides/image-4.png)
</Frame>
