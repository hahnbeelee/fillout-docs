## What is user login?

User login lets you control who can access your app. With Zite, you get a secure, ready-made login page, easy user management, and flexible access controls—all with no coding required.

## How to Add User Login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Type 'Add user login'">
    In your Zite chat, type <b>Add user login</b>. Zite will automatically build a full user login backend and add it to your app.
    <Frame>
      ![Zite chat with 'Add user login' prompt](/images/auth-step-1.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="View your new login page">
    Zite instantly creates a login screen for your app—no code needed.
    <Frame>
      ![Zite login page preview](/images/auth-step-2.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your login page">
    Click <b>Edit</b> to personalize your login page. Change the logo, background, and text, or ask AI to make edits for you.
    <Frame>
      ![Zite login page customization](/images/auth-step-3.png)
    </Frame>
    <Tip>
      Use your brand colors and logo for a professional look.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage users">
    Open the <b>Users</b> tab to view, edit, or delete users. Manage access with just a few clicks.
    <Frame>
      ![Zite user management tab](/images/auth-step-4.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Control access and login methods">
    Choose how users log in: Magic link or Google login.  
    Next, set who can access your app:
    1. Invite-only
    2. Open to anyone
    3. Only people from your company
    <Frame>
      ![Zite access control settings](/images/auth-step-5.png)
    </Frame>
    <Note>
      For company-only access, enter your domain and save your settings.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
