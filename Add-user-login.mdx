## How to add user login to your apps in seconds

<Steps>
  <Step title="Type 'Add user login' into the chat">
    It's that simple, wait for Zite to complete its work and user login will be added to your app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Zite creates a login page for your user to login">
    No coding required.

    <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/maf1.webp)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="View and manage users">
    Click `Users` and you can view and manage users for your app.

<Frame>
  ![Customize the design](/images/maf2.webp)
</Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose who can access your app">
   Pick who gets access to your app: invite-only, open to anyone or only people from your company.
<Frame>
  ![Add questions or fields](/images/maf3.webp)
</Frame>
  </Step>

That’s it\! Your app now has user login and a full backend in less than 30 seconds!
</Steps>
