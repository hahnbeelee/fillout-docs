## How does it work?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Type Add user login">
    Zite will get to work building out a full user login backend and add it to your app.
     <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/auth-step-1.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Login page created">
    Zite instantly creates a login screen. No code needed.

    <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/auth-step-2.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize the login">
    Click Edit and customize your login page, you can customize the logo, background and text, or tell AI what you want and it will make the edits for you!

     <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/auth-step-3.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage users">
    The Users tab allows you to view and manage users. You can edit and delete users from here.
  <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/auth-step-4.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Control access">
   Choose how you want your users to be able to login: Magic link or by Google login. 
   
   Next choose who gets access to your app: 
   1. Invite-only
   2. Open to anyone 
   3. Only people from your company.
  <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/auth-step-5.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
